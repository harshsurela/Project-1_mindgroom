Instructions

  • THIS APPLICATION WILL USE YOUR PHONE'S CAMERA AND FLASHLIGHT TO TAKE YOUR FINGERPRINT SCANS.
  • USE A PLAIN WHITE PAPER AS BASE AND KEEP YOUR FINGER ABOVE THE PAPER AND CLICK.
  • ENSURE THAT THERE IS NO SHADOW OF THE FINGERPRINT WHILE CLICKING THE IMAGE.
  • HOLD YOUR FINGER 5 INCHES AWAY FROM THE PHONE'S CAMERA .
  • JUST KEEP THE TOP 1/3RD PORTION OF YOUR RESPECTUIVE FINGER INSIDE THE INNERCIRCLE IN THE APP SCREEN WHILE TAKING FINGERPRINT IMAGES.
  • IF YOU HAVE A NATURALLY WET SKIN PLEASE APPLY TALCUM POWDER TO MAKE IT DRY.
  • IF YOU HAVE VERY DRY SKIN PLEASE APPLY MOISTURIZER TO MAKE IT LITTLE WET.
